FAQs

Welcome to the Comprehensive RatingRocker FAQs
Membership

1. How can I reset my password?

You have the option to reset your RatingRocker website password whether you're logged in or logged out. If you're logged in, navigate to 'My Account' and choose 'View Account.' Scroll down, click 'Change Password,' and follow the prompts. Remember that your new password should be 8 to 32 characters long, including at least one letter and one number or symbol (e.g., !\"$%^&*()_+=-). To reset your password while logged out, click here and provide your registered email address. We'll send you a password reset link to your email. Follow the link and enter your new password twice to complete the process.

2. How do I become a member of RatingRocker?

You can become a member of RatingRocker online. Just head over to our platform and register with your information.

3. How can I cancel my membership?

We're sorry to see you go, but if you wish to cancel your membership, simply log into your 'My Account' and navigate to the cancellation section. Follow the steps provided there.

4. What kinds of products does RatingRocker offer?

RatingRocker provides a wide array of products and advice to assist you in making significant life decisions and tackling important moments. Learn more about what we offer on our dedicated page.

Scams

5. How can I report a scam?

If you've come across a scam that you'd like to report, we encourage you to use our scam sharer tool. Sharing your experience will contribute to our research, content creation, and policy work aimed at investigating scams. Additionally, make sure to report scams to the relevant authorities. Refer to this guide for guidance.

6. How can I verify the authenticity of an email from RatingRocker?

Genuine emails from RatingRocker will be sent from the address contact@ratingrocker.com. The sender's name will prominently feature "RatingRocker," such as "RatingRocker," or "RatingRocker Campaigns." If you ever receive an email that seems suspicious, reach out to us immediately.

Technical Issues

7. What should I do if I encounter a technical issue on the website?

If you come across a technical issue while browsing our website, you can report it using the contact form, choose 'Technical Issue as Title' and describe the problem in the comments box. Once you're done, hit 'Submit.'

8. What happens to archived product reviews?

At RatingRocker, we invest our resources in testing areas that provide the most value to consumers. Occasionally, we decide to discontinue reporting certain types of products. While we keep these reviews on our website, they are archived when the information becomes outdated.
