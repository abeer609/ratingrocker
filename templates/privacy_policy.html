Do we employ cookies?



Our utilization of cookies and analogous technologies serves the purpose of enhancing delivery, optimization, personalization, and analysis of our services, alongside our advertising initiatives. We harness an amalgamation of cookies and other progressive technologies, such as pixels and tracking codes, to amass information in alignment with the objectives elucidated in this policy.

So, what exactly is a cookie?

In simple terms, a cookie represents a diminutive text file that gets stored through your web browser on your computer or device, upon the request of the websites you frequent or the applications you employ. These cookies encapsulate your preferences and settings, which play a pivotal role in furnishing us with the requisite insights to ensure an unwaveringly pertinent and harmonious user experience. Furthermore, cookies aid us in documenting details about experiments or novel enhancements we may experiment with, thus enabling us to scrutinize your usage patterns and gauge their efficacy. Our website's cookies may be established by us, our collaborative third-party partners, or independent entities, including advertisers.

What about pixels, you ask?

Pixels manifest as unobtrusive, translucent image files imbedded within web pages or emails. These unassuming entities serve the purpose of decoding your interactions with our services, encompassing marketing emails as well.

And what role do tracking codes play?

Tracking codes are segments of code strategically integrated within pages to quantify variables like visits and engagements. Employing tracking codes empowers us to delve deeper into your interactions with our services, the advertisements you encounter, and, on a broader scale, your engagement with our platform. For a more comprehensive understanding, I encourage you to peruse our Cookie Policy, which delves into finer nuances and provides a detailed breakdown of the assorted cookie types and akin technologies we employ.
